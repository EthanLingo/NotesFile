# Julia数组重复





`repeat()`举例

```julia
julia> repeat(1:2, inner=2)
4-element Vector{Int64}:
 1
 1
 2
 2

julia> repeat(1:2, outer=2)
4-element Vector{Int64}:
 1
 2
 1
 2

julia> repeat([1 2; 3 4], inner=(2, 1), outer=(1, 3))
4×6 Matrix{Int64}:
 1  2  1  2  1  2
 1  2  1  2  1  2
 3  4  3  4  3  4
 3  4  3  4  3  4
```
